KRONOS

I set aside my ambition and fill a pine box with stone

Kronos blowing notes on a ticking horn

Bespeckled with tiny moving hands

Playing songs of birthed and reborn

 

I’m captivated by the moon violently setting

In the bottomless chasm of sleep

As my crate is swallowed by the pit

Now forever the tomb’s to keep

 

Overtones rot the wood away

While stones replace the falling rain

I find another box to fill

As Kronos plays his song again
